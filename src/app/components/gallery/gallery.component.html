The dioceses in Africa reflect the vast structure and growth of the Catholic Church across the continent, organized into hundreds of local churches within different countries and regions. Each diocese, led by a bishop or archbishop, serves as a unified network under SECAM that strengthens the mission of the Church in Africa.
